Our Story

Crafting Memories

Djm events was founded with a singular belief: that every occasion deserves to be celebrated with beauty, intention, and flawless execution. From a small team of passionate planners, we've grown into one of New York's most trusted event management companies.

Over 15 years and 850+ events later, we continue to pour the same love and dedication into every project — whether it's an intimate dinner for 20 or a grand gala for 2,000.
